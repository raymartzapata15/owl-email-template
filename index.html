How It Works

Each OWL Learning System course is created, owned and managed by the instructor(s) and admin(s). The foundation of each OWL Learning course are its lectures which can include videos, presentations, contents, and tests. In addition, instructors and admin can add resources and various types of practice activities, as a way to enhance the learning experience of learners.

Once a test and/or a course has been finished, learners can acquire badges and certificates of participation. Also, if you fail the course, you will have to retake it to get the certificate (3 times maximum attempts.) If you pass, you will be endorsed to ServiceFirst, Inc Recruitment Team for an interview!
